Atherosclerosis

What is it?

It's a buildup of cholesterol plaque in the walls of arteries, causing obstruction of blood flow.

Causes of it

  • High cholesterol
  • High blood pressure
  • Diabetes
  • Certain genetic influences.

Symptoms of it

  • Sudden Arm/Legacy Weakness
  • Difficulty speaking or slurring.
  • Temporary vision loss.
  • Drooping facial muscles.

How to lower the risk

  • Quit Smoking
  • Diet
  • Exercise

Treatment for it

A healthy diet and exercise can alleviate blocked arteries, while treatments may include medication, artery-opening procedures, and surgery.
